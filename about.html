About Hello Web

Hello Web is a digital literacy campaign to inspire young minds from across the globe to demystify the web, understand its basic function and contribute to the development of a revolution that has changed so many lives in such a less time.

The Web is an essential component of the cosmopolitan and metropolitan population in all countries but relatively few things are known about the functioning of this vital resource especially in under resourced areas of developing nations, which constitute a great part of the world population.

Unless - people are educated about the web in particular and technology in general they will not be able to utilise the web to its maximum potential which involves active usage and contribution by all.
