Stories: The Path Traversed

Touching Lives
Girl 2B Home: June 18, 2016

Shreenath, a volunteer from #HelloWeb, was touched by the zeal and enthusiasm of the children at Hope Foundation. If these children were provided the same quality of education as available at some of the most elite of our institutions, these children would know no limits. We are trying to bring in the Web as a quality amplifier when it comes to their education.

Small initiatives bring big changes
Hope Computer Training Centre: June 20, 2016

#HelloWeb is not just about increasing web literacy and the use of digital technologies as a quality amplifier but also about informing the common masses of the new age technologies which is making lives simpler across the world. We look forward to integrating the Web in the day to day Indian life across all strata of Indian society.

Hello Web reaches Gujarat
Surat: June, 2013

Ashun is young college goer from Gujarat. He wished to pass on his knowledge of experience to these orphaned kids so that they may become game changers in their own right, in their own fields no matter what that be. With #HelloWeb he is doing just that, he is helping shape up the next generation of professionals unfazed by their dark and humble beginnings.

Helping them dream big
Nabadisha: June 22, 2016

Students and individuals from all walks of life have contributed to #HelloWeb so far. The number of individuals that come forward to do their bit for the society brings high hopes for our generation. Together, the aim of increasing web literacy count in India and providing technological solutions to the problems faced in India, does not seem like a very hard task.

Web - How does it Help?
Punorjibon - Rehabilitation Home for Boys: June, 2016

It’s not just about teaching the children about the basic usage of the Web. #HelloWeb is about making the Web an integral part of our lives. We aim to touch the lives of people by making them a part of our dreams, so that someday, little by little we may bring about a change we have all wanted to see. For this our sessions focus on personality and ethics development as well.

#HelloWeb, a student initiative to drive Web Literacy count, to enhance integration of Web and it’s technologies in daily lives, to tackle common issues faced by the common man with uncommon and unconventional technologies and to shape up a generation of responsible and dynamic global citizens.
